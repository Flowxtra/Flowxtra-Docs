# Creating an Account

Get started with Flowxtra by creating your account. This guide will walk you through the registration process and initial setup.

[Create Your Account →](https://my.flowxtra.com/registration)

## Prerequisites

Before creating your account, make sure you have:

- A valid business email address (personal emails like Gmail or Yahoo are not accepted)
- Basic information about your company
- Commercial registration number or tax ID (if available)

<Callout type="info">
We recommend using your work email address with your company domain for faster verification and better security.
</Callout>

## Account Creation Process

<Steps>
<Step>
### Company Information

The first step covers your company's basic information.

**Company Name**:
- Enter your organization's legal name
- This will be displayed on your job postings and company page

**Company Website:**
- Must start with `https://`
- Used for verification and displayed on your company profile

**Location:**
- Select your country and city
- Helps candidates find local opportunities
- Used for regional job board distribution

**Industry**:
- Select your company's primary industry from the dropdown
- This helps us categorize your job postings correctly

**Data Center Location**:
Choose where your data will be stored:

- **Europe**: For GDPR compliance and European companies
- **United States**: For US-based companies

<Callout type="warning">
**Important**: The data center location cannot be changed after registration. Choose based on your company's regulatory requirements and data sovereignty needs.
</Callout>

All your company and candidate data will be stored in your selected data center region to ensure compliance with local data protection regulations.
</Step>

<Step>
### User Account

Enter your personal information to create your admin account.

**Personal Information**:
- **First Name**: Your given name
- **Last Name**: Your family name
- **Work Email**: Your business email address
- **Phone Number**: Contact phone number

<Callout type="danger">
**Work Email Required**: Free email providers (Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook.com, etc.) are not accepted. You must use your company domain email (e.g., john@yourcompany.com).
</Callout>

**Password**:
Create a strong, secure password with:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (recommended: !@#$%^&*)

**Terms and Conditions**:
- Read and accept the Terms of Service
- Read and accept the Privacy Policy
- Both must be accepted to proceed

Click **Create Account** to continue.
</Step>

<Step>
### Email Verification

Verify your email address to activate your account.

1. Check your email inbox for a verification message from Flowxtra
2. The email will contain a **6-digit verification code**
3. Enter the code in the verification field
4. Click **Verify** to proceed

<Callout type="info">
**Didn't receive the code?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Click "Resend Code" to request a new one
- Ensure you entered the correct email address
</Callout>

The verification code expires after 15 minutes. If it expires, request a new code.
</Step>

<Step>
### Complete Your Profile

Finalize your company profile to start using Flowxtra.

**Company Logo**:
- Upload your company logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
- Recommended size: 400x400 pixels
- Maximum file size: 5MB

**Company Details**:
- **Number of Employees**: Select your company size range
- **Imprint URL**: Link to your legal imprint page containing commercial registration or tax ID
- **Contact Email**: General company email for inquiries
- **Phone Number**: Company phone number

<Callout type="info">
**What is an Imprint?**
An Imprint (Impressum) is a legal requirement in many countries (especially in Europe) that includes:
- Commercial registration number
- Tax ID / VAT number
- Legal company name and address
- Contact details

This helps verify your company's legitimacy and speeds up the approval process.
</Callout>

**About Your Company**:
- **Company Description**: A brief overview of what your company does (2-3 sentences)
- **About Team**: Describe your team culture and values
- **Mission Statement**: Your company's vision or mission

Click **Complete Setup** to finish.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Account Review Process

<Callout type="warning">
After completing registration, your account may undergo a brief review process to ensure platform quality and prevent spam.
</Callout>

**Review Timeline**:
- **Established Companies**: Usually approved within hours if you have:
  - Valid commercial registration number
  - Tax ID / VAT number
  - Established company website
  - Verified domain email

- **New Companies**: May take up to 1 business day
  - We may contact you to verify company information
  - This helps us maintain quality and prevent fraudulent accounts

**During Review**:
- You can access your account and set up your workspace
- Job postings may require approval before going live
- Full features will be unlocked after verification

Once verified, future job postings will be approved automatically without manual review.

## Security Best Practices

### Password Security

- Use a unique password for Flowxtra
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) after login
- Change your password regularly (every 90 days recommended)
- Never share your password with anyone
- Use a password manager for secure storage

### Account Security

- Review login activity regularly in Settings
- Log out from shared or public devices
- Use secure networks (avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive operations)
- Enable session timeout in Settings
- Set up security notifications for suspicious activity

### Data Protection

- Review privacy settings in your account
- Control who can see your company data
- Understand data retention policies
- Know your data export and deletion rights
- Read our [Privacy Policy](https://flowxtra.com/en/privacy-policy)

## Common Issues

### Email Verification Not Received

**Solutions**:
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add `noreply@flowxtra.com` to your contacts
3. Ensure your email server isn't blocking our emails
4. Request a new verification code
5. Contact support@flowxtra.com if the issue persists

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check email for the reset link
5. Create a new password
6. Log in with your new credentials

### Account Already Exists

If you see "Email already registered":

1. Try logging in instead of signing up
2. Use the password reset if you forgot your password
3. Check if you registered with a different email
4. Contact support if you need help accessing your account

### Company Name Already Taken

If your company name or subdomain is taken:

1. Add a location suffix (e.g., "Acme Corp Berlin")
2. Include a descriptor (e.g., "Acme Software Solutions")
3. Use your legal entity name
4. Contact support@flowxtra.com to verify ownership if it's legitimately your company

### Work Email Required

If your email is rejected:

**Not Accepted**:
- Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail, Outlook.com
- Any free email provider

**Accepted**:
- Company domain emails (e.g., john@yourcompany.com)
- Business email addresses

**Solution**:
- Use your work email with your company domain
- If you don't have one, contact your IT department
- Contact support if you have a special case

## Data Rights & Privacy

### Data Storage Location

Your data is stored in the data center you selected during registration:
- **Europe**: Frankfurt, Germany (GDPR compliant)
- **United States**: US East region

All candidate and company data remains in your selected region for data sovereignty and compliance.

### Request Your Data

You can request a copy of all your data:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy Settings**
2. Click **Request Data Export**
3. Select data types to export
4. Receive download link via email within 24 hours

### Delete Your Account

To deactivate your account:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy Settings**
2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
3. Click **Delete Account**
4. Confirm the action

<Callout type="warning">
**Note**: "Delete Account" means temporary deactivation. Your profile is hidden and access is blocked, but data is retained for 30 days to allow account recovery. For permanent deletion, contact support@flowxtra.com.
</Callout>

Before deleting:
- Export your data if needed
- Close all active job postings
- Notify your team members
- Cancel any active subscriptions

For more information, see our [Privacy Policy](https://flowxtra.com/en/privacy-policy) and [Terms of Use](https://flowxtra.com/en/terms-of-use).
