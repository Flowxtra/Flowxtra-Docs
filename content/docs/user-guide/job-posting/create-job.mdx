# Creating a Job Posting

This guide will walk you through creating a job posting in Flowxtra.

## Getting Started

To create a new job posting:

1. Navigate to the Jobs dashboard
2. Click "Create New Job"
3. Fill in the required information
4. Publish your job

## Next Steps

Once you've created your job, you can customize it further by adding more details.
