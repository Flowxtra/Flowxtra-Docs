# Salary & Benefits

Transparent compensation information attracts more qualified candidates and builds trust from the start.

## Salary Configuration

Flowxtra allows flexible salary configurations to match your compensation model.

### Salary Type

Choose how the salary is structured:
- **Fixed**: A precise annual or hourly figure (e.g., $80,000/year)
- **Range**: A minimum and maximum (e.g., $70,000 - $90,000/year)
- **Unspecified**: Salary will not be shown publicly (Not Recommended)

### Pay Period
- Yearly
- Monthly
- Bi-weekly
- Weekly
- Hourly

### Currency
Select the currency for the position (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.).

<Callout type="warn">
**Google Jobs Optimization**: Google strongly prioritizes job listings with salary information. Providing a salary range significantly increases your visibility on Google for Jobs.
</Callout>

## Benefits

Select the benefits offered with this role. Flowxtra provides standard categories:

### Health & Wellness
- Medical Insurance
- Dental Insurance
- Vision Insurance
- Mental Health Benefits
- Gym Membership

### Financial
- 401(k) / Retirement Plan
- Performance Bonus
- Stock Options / Equity
- Signing Bonus

### Work-Life Balance
- Unlimited PTO
- Flexible Hours
- Remote Work Options
- Parental Leave

### Professional Growth
- Learning Budget
- Conference Attendance
- Mentorship Program

## Custom Benefits

If you offer unique perks not listed above (e.g., "Dog-friendly office", "Weekly team lunches"), you can add them manually:

1. Scroll to the **Custom Benefits** section
2. Type the benefit name
3. Click **Add** or press Enter
