# Company Profile

Your company profile is the face of your organization on Flowxtra. A complete, professional profile attracts better candidates and improves your employer brand.

## Why Company Profile Matters

A well-crafted company profile:

- Increases application rates by up to 40%
- Improves candidate quality
- Enhances employer branding
- Boosts SEO for job postings
- Builds trust with candidates

## Accessing Company Profile

Navigate to **Settings** > **Company Profile** or click **Edit Profile** on your career page.

## Basic Information

### Company Name

Your official company name:

- Use legal business name
- Avoid abbreviations unless commonly used
- Include legal entity type if relevant (Inc., LLC, GmbH)

**Example**: "Acme Corporation Inc."

### Company Logo

Upload your company logo:

**Requirements**:
- Format: PNG, JPG, or SVG
- Size: Minimum 200x200px
- Max file size: 2MB
- Square or horizontal orientation

**Best Practices**:
- Use high-resolution image
- Transparent background (PNG)
- Include brand colors
- Ensure readability at small sizes

<Callout type="info">
Your logo appears on job postings, career page, and application forms.
</Callout>

### Tagline

A brief, compelling tagline (max 100 characters):

**Good Examples**:
- "Building the future of cloud computing"
- "Where innovation meets excellence"
- "Transforming healthcare through technology"

**Avoid**:
- Generic phrases ("We're hiring!")
- Overly long descriptions
- Buzzword overload

### Industry

Select your primary industry:

- Technology
- Healthcare
- Finance
- Education
- Manufacturing
- Retail
- Consulting
- Other

This helps candidates find relevant opportunities.

### Company Size

Indicate your organization size:

- 1-10 employees
- 11-50 employees
- 51-200 employees
- 201-500 employees
- 501-1000 employees
- 1000+ employees

## Company Description

### About Us

Write a compelling company overview (500-1000 words):

**Structure**:

1. **Opening** (2-3 sentences)
   - What you do
   - Your mission
   - Your impact

2. **History** (1-2 paragraphs)
   - When founded
   - Key milestones
   - Growth story

3. **Products/Services** (1-2 paragraphs)
   - What you offer
   - Who you serve
   - Your unique value

4. **Culture** (1-2 paragraphs)
   - Work environment
   - Values
   - Team dynamics

5. **Future** (1 paragraph)
   - Vision
   - Growth plans
   - Opportunities

**Example**:

```
At TechCorp, we're revolutionizing how businesses manage their data. 
Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small startup to a team of 200+ 
professionals serving over 10,000 customers worldwide.

Our cloud-based platform helps companies of all sizes store, analyze, 
and act on their data in real-time. We combine cutting-edge technology 
with intuitive design to make data management accessible to everyone.

We're a team of passionate innovators who believe in work-life balance, 
continuous learning, and making a positive impact. Our culture is built 
on transparency, collaboration, and celebrating wins together.

As we continue to grow, we're looking for talented individuals who share 
our vision and want to help shape the future of data management.
```

### Mission Statement

Your company's mission (1-2 sentences):

**Example**: "To empower businesses with data-driven insights that drive growth and innovation."

### Values

List your core company values (3-6 values):

**Format**:
- **Value Name**: Brief description

**Example**:
- **Innovation**: We embrace new ideas and technologies
- **Integrity**: We do the right thing, always
- **Collaboration**: We achieve more together
- **Excellence**: We strive for the highest quality
- **Impact**: We make a difference

## Contact Information

### Website

Your company website URL:

- Use full URL with https://
- Link to homepage or about page
- Ensure website is mobile-friendly

### Email

General company email:

- Use professional email (info@, hello@, careers@)
- Avoid personal emails
- Monitor regularly for inquiries

### Phone

Company phone number:

- Include country code
- Use format: +1 (555) 123-4567
- Ensure someone answers during business hours

### Address

Company headquarters address:

- Street address
- City
- State/Province
- Postal code
- Country

**Multiple Locations**:
Add additional offices in **Locations** section.

## Common Mistakes

### Incomplete Profile

Avoid:
- Missing logo
- No company description
- Empty photo gallery
- No contact information

### Generic Content

Avoid:
- Stock photos
- Corporate jargon
- Vague descriptions
- Copy-paste content

### Outdated Information

Avoid:
- Old employee count
- Closed office locations
- Discontinued products
- Obsolete benefits
