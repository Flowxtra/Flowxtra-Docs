# Creating an Account

Get started with Flowxtra by creating your account. This guide will walk you through the registration process and initial setup.

## Prerequisites

Before creating your account, make sure you have:

- A valid business email address
- Basic information about your company
- A secure password in mind

<Callout type="info">
We recommend using your work email address rather than a personal email for better organization and security.
</Callout>

## Account Creation Process

<Steps>
<Step>
### Visit the Sign-Up Page

1. Navigate to [flowxtra.com](https://flowxtra.com)
2. Click the **Sign Up** button in the top right corner
3. You'll be directed to the registration form
</Step>

<Step>
### Enter Your Information

Fill in the required fields:

#### Personal Information

- **Full Name**: Your first and last name
- **Email Address**: Your work email
- **Password**: Create a strong password

**Password Requirements**:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (recommended)

<Callout type="warn">
Choose a strong, unique password. Avoid using the same password you use for other services.
</Callout>

#### Company Information

- **Company Name**: Your organization's legal name
- **Industry**: Select from dropdown
- **Company Size**: Number of employees
- **Job Title**: Your role in the company

#### Terms and Conditions

- Read the Terms of Service
- Read the Privacy Policy
- Check the box to agree to both

Click **Create Account** to proceed.
</Step>

<Step>
### Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox for a verification message from Flowxtra
2. Click the **Verify Email** link in the email
3. You'll be redirected to the Flowxtra dashboard

<Callout type="info">
**Didn't receive the email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Click "Resend Verification Email" on the dashboard
</Callout>

The verification link expires after 24 hours. If it expires, request a new one from your dashboard.
</Step>

<Step>
### Complete Your Profile

After email verification, complete your profile:

1. **Upload Profile Picture** (optional but recommended)
2. **Set Your Timezone**: For accurate scheduling
3. **Choose Notification Preferences**: Email and in-app notifications
4. **Select Language**: Interface language (English, German, Arabic)

Click **Save and Continue** to proceed to company setup.
</Step>

<Step>
### Set Up Your Company Profile

Configure your company information:

#### Basic Information

- **Company Logo**: Upload your logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
- **Company Description**: Brief overview of your company
- **Website URL**: Your company website
- **Founded Year**: When your company was established

#### Contact Information

- **Phone Number**: Main company phone
- **Email**: General company email
- **Address**: Company headquarters address

#### Social Media

Add links to your social media profiles:
- LinkedIn company page
- Twitter/X account
- Facebook page
- Instagram account

Click **Save Company Profile** to finish setup.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Account Types

### Free Trial

All new accounts start with a 14-day free trial:

- Full access to all features
- Unlimited job postings
- Up to 100 applications
- No credit card required

### Paid Plans

After the trial, choose from our plans:

- **Starter**: For small teams (1-10 employees)
- **Professional**: For growing companies (11-50 employees)
- **Business**: For established companies (51-200 employees)
- **Enterprise**: For large organizations (200+ employees)

See [Subscription & Billing](/docs/user-guide/account/subscription-billing) for details.

## Security Best Practices

### Password Security

- Use a unique password for Flowxtra
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Change your password regularly
- Never share your password

### Account Security

- Review login activity regularly
- Log out from shared devices
- Use secure networks (avoid public Wi-Fi)
- Enable session timeout

### Data Protection

- Review privacy settings
- Control who can see your data
- Understand data retention policies
- Know your data export options

## Common Issues

### Email Verification Not Received

**Solutions**:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Add noreply@flowxtra.com to your contacts
3. Request a new verification email
4. Contact support if issue persists

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password** on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create a new password
5. Log in with new password

### Account Already Exists

If you see "Email already registered":

1. Try logging in instead of signing up
2. Use password reset if you forgot password
3. Check if you have multiple email addresses
4. Contact support for help

### Company Name Already Taken

If your company name is taken:

1. Add a location (e.g., "Acme Corp - New York")
2. Include a descriptor (e.g., "Acme Corp - Software")
3. Use your legal entity name
4. Contact support to verify ownership

## Deleting Your Account

If you need to delete your account:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Account**
2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
3. Click **Delete Account**
4. Confirm deletion

<Callout type="warn">
**Warning**: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including job postings and applications, will be deleted.
</Callout>

Before deleting:
- Export your data
- Close all active job postings
- Notify your team members
- Cancel your subscription
