# Subscription & Billing

Manage your Flowxtra subscription, payment methods, and billing information. This guide covers plans, pricing, and billing management.

## Subscription Plans

- Full access to all features

- No credit card required
- Cancel anytime

**Features**:

- Standard integrations

**Price**: $49/month or $470/year (save 20%)

- All integrations

**Price**: $149/month or $1,430/year (save 20%)

## Managing Subscription

### View Current Plan

Check your plan in **Settings** > **Subscription**:

- Current plan name
- Billing cycle
- Next billing date
- Usage statistics
- Plan limits

### Upgrade Plan

To upgrade your plan:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select new plan
4. Choose billing cycle (monthly/yearly)
5. Review changes
6. Click **Confirm Upgrade**

**What happens**:
- Immediate access to new features
- Prorated billing for current period
- New limits apply immediately

<Callout type="info">
Upgrades are prorated. You only pay the difference for the remaining billing period.
</Callout>

### Downgrade Plan

To downgrade your plan:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select lower plan
4. Review limitations
5. Click **Confirm Downgrade**

**What happens**:
- Takes effect at end of billing period
- Keep current features until then
- Credit applied to next bill
- May need to reduce active jobs

<Callout type="warn">
Ensure you're within new plan limits before downgrading (e.g., reduce active jobs if needed).
</Callout>

### Cancel Subscription

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

**What happens**:
- Access continues until end of billing period
- No further charges
- Data retained for 30 days
- Can reactivate within 30 days

## Billing Management

### Payment Methods

#### Add Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter card details:
   - Card number
   - Expiration date
   - CVV
   - Billing address
4. Click **Save Card**

**Supported Cards**:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

#### Update Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Update** next to card
3. Enter new card details
4. Click **Save**

#### Remove Payment Method

1. Must have another payment method first
2. Click **Remove** next to card
3. Confirm removal

### Billing Information

Update billing details:

**Company Information**:
- Legal company name
- Tax ID / VAT number
- Billing address
- Contact email

**Invoice Preferences**:
- Invoice email recipients
- PO number (optional)
- Custom notes

### Invoices

#### View Invoices

Access all invoices in **Settings** > **Billing** > **Invoices**:

- Invoice number
- Date
- Amount
- Status (Paid, Pending, Failed)
- Download PDF

#### Download Invoice

1. Click invoice number
2. Click **Download PDF**
3. Save to your computer

Invoices include:
- Itemized charges
- Tax breakdown
- Payment method
- Billing period

### Billing History

View complete billing history:

- All charges
- Refunds
- Credits
- Failed payments
- Plan changes

## Usage and Limits

### Monitor Usage

Track your usage in **Settings** > **Subscription** > **Usage**:

**Current Usage**:
- Active jobs: X / limit
- Applications this month: X / limit
- API calls: X / limit
- Storage used: X / limit

**Usage Trends**:
- Daily/weekly/monthly graphs
- Historical data
- Projected usage

### Overage Handling

**What happens when you exceed limits**:

**Active Jobs**:
- Cannot create new jobs
- Must close or delete existing jobs
- Or upgrade plan

**Applications**:
- Soft limit with overage fees
- $0.50 per additional application
- Or upgrade to higher plan

**API Calls**:
- Rate limiting applied
- Upgrade for higher limits

<Callout type="warn">
Monitor usage regularly to avoid service interruptions or unexpected charges.
</Callout>

## Billing Cycles

### Monthly Billing

- Charged every month
- Cancel anytime
- Flexible for short-term needs
- Higher total cost

### Annual Billing

- Charged once per year
- Save 20% vs monthly
- Best for long-term use
- Commitment required

### Switching Billing Cycles

Change from monthly to annual:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **Switch to Annual**
3. Review savings
4. Confirm switch

**What happens**:
- Immediate charge for annual plan
- Credit for unused monthly time
- New annual renewal date

## Payments and Charges

### Automatic Billing

Payments are processed automatically:

- On your billing date
- Using default payment method
- Email receipt sent
- Invoice generated

### Failed Payments

If payment fails:

1. Email notification sent
2. Retry after 3 days
3. Second retry after 7 days
4. Account suspended after 14 days

**To resolve**:
- Update payment method
- Contact your bank
- Pay manually
- Contact support

### Refunds

**Refund Policy**:

- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Annual plans: Prorated refund
- Monthly plans: No refund for current month
- Overage charges: Non-refundable

**Request Refund**:

1. Contact support@flowxtra.com
2. Provide account details
3. Explain reason
4. Refund processed in 5-7 business days

## Tax and Compliance

### Sales Tax

Sales tax applied based on:

- Billing address
- Local tax laws
- Business vs personal account

### VAT (EU)

For EU customers:

- Provide VAT number
- Reverse charge if valid
- VAT shown on invoices

### Tax Exemption

If tax-exempt:

1. Contact support
2. Provide exemption certificate
3. Update billing settings

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Declined

If payment is declined:

1. Verify card details
2. Check card balance
3. Contact your bank
4. Try different card
5. Contact support

### Billing Discrepancy

If you notice incorrect charges:

1. Review invoice details
2. Check usage statistics
3. Contact support with:
   - Invoice number
   - Expected amount
   - Actual amount
   - Explanation

### Cannot Cancel

If unable to cancel:

- Must be account Owner
- Resolve outstanding balance
- Contact support for help
