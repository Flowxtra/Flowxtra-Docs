# Analytics & Reporting

Data-driven hiring leads to better results. The Analytics dashboard provides insights into your recruitment performance.

## Overview Dashboard

Access via **Reports** in the sidebar.

### Key Metrics
- **Job Views**: Total traffic to your listings.
- **Application Rate**: Percentage of viewers who apply.
- **Time to Hire**: Average days from "Job Posted" to "Hired".
- **Pipeline Health**: Number of active candidates in each stage.

## Source Reporting

Where are your best candidates coming from?
- **Organic**: Google, Bing
- **Direct**: Your website / widget
- **Social**: LinkedIn, Twitter
- **Job Boards**: Referrals from aggregators
- **Referrals**: Employee referral links

Use this to decide where to spend advertising budget.

## Exporting Reports

Need to share data with stakeholders?

1. Click **Export** in the top right
2. Choose **Date Range** (Last 30 Days, YTD, Custom)
3. Choose **Format**: CSV or PDF
4. Download

<Callout type="info">
**Privacy**: Reports contain aggregated data. To export raw candidate data, use the "Export Candidates" option in the Jobs dashboard.
</Callout>
