# Content Guidelines

To maintain quality and safety, Flowxtra prohibits certain types of content.

## Prohibited Content

- **Discriminatory Language**: Race, religion, gender, age, etc.
- **Misleading Jobs**: Multi-level marketing (MLM), "Get rich quick" schemes.
- **Adult Content**: Sexual services or explicit material.
- **Unpaid Work**: Unless clearly marked as a legal internship.

## Compliance

### Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO)
We recommend adding an EEO statement to your footer or description:
> "We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity at our company."

## Consequences
Violation of these guidelines may result in:
- Job removal
- Account suspension
- IP ban
