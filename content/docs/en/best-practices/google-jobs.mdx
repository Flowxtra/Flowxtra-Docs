## What is Google for Jobs?

Google for Jobs is a job search feature integrated directly into Google Search. When job seekers search for positions on Google, your job posting can appear in:

- **Google Search Results**: With rich snippets showing key details
- **Google Jobs Widget**: A dedicated job search interface
- **Google Maps**: Location-based job searches

<Callout type="info">
Jobs published to Google for Jobs get **3-5x more visibility** compared to traditional job boards, reaching candidates actively searching on Google.
</Callout>

## How to Publish to Google for Jobs

<Steps>
<Step>
### Create or Edit Your Job

1. Go to **Recruitment** > **Jobs**
2. Either create a new job or click **Edit** on an existing one
3. Ensure all required information is complete:
   - Job title
   - Description (at least 200 characters)
   - Location
   - Employment type
   - Salary information (recommended)

<Callout type="tip">
Complete job postings with salary information perform better on Google for Jobs.
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step>
### Navigate to Job Boards Tab

1. Click on the **Job Boards** tab at the top of the edit page
2. You'll see a list of available job boards
3. Look for **"Google for Jobs"** in the list

<Callout type="info">
**Automatic Publishing**: When you publish to Google for Jobs, your job is automatically published to **two locations**:

1. **Flowxtra.com** - The main Flowxtra job board (Publisher: flowxtra.com)
2. **Your Company Career Page** - Your unique subdomain (e.g., `yourcompany.flowxtra.com`)

Both locations have their own sitemap that Google automatically crawls and indexes.
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step>
### Publish to Google

1. Check the box next to **"Google for Jobs"**
2. Review the preview to see how your job will appear
3. Click **"Publish"** button
4. Wait for confirmation message

**What Happens Automatically:**

When you click publish, your job is instantly published to:

- **flowxtra.com/jobs** - The main Flowxtra job board
  - Publisher: flowxtra.com
  - Visible to all job seekers browsing Flowxtra
  
- **yourcompany.flowxtra.com/jobs** - Your company's career page
  - Publisher: yourcompany.flowxtra.com
  - Your unique subdomain chosen during registration
  - Branded with your company logo and information

**Google Indexing:**

Each company subdomain has its own XML sitemap:
- Located at: `yourcompany.flowxtra.com/sitemap.xml`
- Google automatically crawls this sitemap
- Jobs are indexed and appear in Google search results
- **Indexing time**: Typically 24-48 hours, but can take up to 72 hours

<Callout type="info">
Google crawls your company's sitemap regularly. Once indexed, any new jobs you publish will be discovered and indexed automatically.
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step>
### Verify Publication

After 24-48 hours:

1. Search on Google: `[Job Title] [Location] site:flowxtra.com`
2. Your job should appear in the results
3. Check that all information displays correctly
4. Test the "Apply" button to ensure it works

</Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements for Google for Jobs

To ensure your job appears on Google, make sure it meets these requirements:

### Essential Information

- ✅ **Job Title**: Clear and descriptive (under 100 characters)
- ✅ **Description**: Detailed (200-10,000 characters)
- ✅ **Location**: Complete address or city/country
- ✅ **Employment Type**: Full-time, Part-time, Contract, etc.
- ✅ **Company Information**: Name and website
- ✅ **Publication Date**: When the job was posted

### Recommended Information

- 💡 **Salary**: Range or exact amount (increases visibility by 30%)
- 💡 **Benefits**: Health insurance, remote work, etc.
- 💡 **Skills**: Required qualifications
- 💡 **Seniority Level**: Junior, Mid-level, Senior, etc.

<Callout type="warn">
**Important**: Jobs without salary information may have lower visibility in Google search results.
</Callout>

## Optimizing for Google for Jobs

### 1. Write Clear Job Titles

**Good Examples:**
- ✅ "Senior Software Engineer - Python"
- ✅ "Marketing Manager - Digital Marketing"
- ✅ "Customer Service Representative - Remote"

**Avoid:**
- ❌ "Rockstar Developer Wanted!"
- ❌ "Ninja Marketer"
- ❌ "Join Our Amazing Team!"

### 2. Include Salary Information

Jobs with salary information get:
- Higher click-through rates
- More qualified applicants
- Better placement in search results

**How to display salary:**
- Use salary ranges for flexibility (e.g., €40,000 - €60,000)
- Specify payment frequency (hourly, monthly, yearly)
- Include currency (EUR, USD, GBP, etc.)

### 3. Write Detailed Descriptions

**Include:**
- Overview of the role and responsibilities
- Required qualifications and skills
- Company culture and values
- Benefits and perks
- Growth opportunities

**Length:** Aim for 300-500 words (Google recommends 200-10,000 characters)

### 4. Specify Location Clearly

**For On-site Jobs:**
- Include full address or at least city and country
- Example: "Berlin, Germany" or "123 Main St, Berlin, Germany"

**For Remote Jobs:**
- Specify if it's global, regional, or country-specific
- Example: "Remote - Europe" or "Remote - Global"

### 5. Add Rich Details

- **Skills**: List 5-10 relevant skills
- **Benefits**: Highlight unique perks
- **Company Logo**: Upload a professional logo
- **Job Image**: Add a custom image for better engagement

## Checking Your Google for Jobs Status

### Using the Check Tab

1. Go to your job's edit page
2. Click on the **Check** tab
3. Look for **"Google Jobs Compliance"** section
4. Review your score and any failed checks

**Score Guide:**
- **90-100%**: Excellent! Your job is fully optimized
- **75-89%**: Good, but some improvements recommended
- **Below 75%**: Needs work for better visibility

### Common Issues and Fixes

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Title too long | Keep under 100 characters |
| Description too short | Write at least 200 characters |
| Missing location | Add complete location information |
| No employment type | Select Full-time, Part-time, etc. |
| Missing salary | Add salary range or exact amount |
| No company website | Add your website in company profile |

## Managing Your Google for Jobs Listing

### Updating Your Job

When you edit your job in Flowxtra:
1. Changes automatically sync to Google for Jobs
2. Updates may take 24-48 hours to appear
3. No need to republish manually

### Removing from Google

To stop showing your job on Google:

1. Go to **Job Boards** tab
2. Uncheck **"Google for Jobs"**
3. Click **"Update"**
4. Job will be removed within 24-48 hours

Alternatively:
- Close the job (stops accepting applications)
- Delete the job (removes completely)

## Tracking Performance

### View Analytics

1. Go to **Recruitment** > **Manage Jobs**
2. Click on your job
3. Click **"Analytics"** button
4. View metrics:
   - **Views**: How many people saw your job
   - **Clicks**: How many clicked to learn more
   - **Applications**: How many applied
   - **Source**: Where candidates came from (including Google)

### Understanding Metrics

- **High views, low clicks**: Improve your job title or summary
- **High clicks, low applications**: Improve job description or requirements
- **Low views overall**: Check Google compliance score and optimize

## Troubleshooting

### My job isn't appearing on Google

**Possible reasons:**

1. **Not enough time passed**: Wait 48 hours after publishing
2. **Low compliance score**: Check the "Check" tab and fix issues
3. **Generic job title**: Use specific, searchable titles
4. **Missing required fields**: Ensure all essential information is complete

**How to fix:**
1. Run the compliance check in the "Check" tab
2. Fix any failed requirements
3. Wait 24-48 hours for Google to re-index
4. Search again using: `[Job Title] [Location] site:flowxtra.com`

### Job appears but with wrong information

1. Edit your job in Flowxtra
2. Save changes
3. Wait 24-48 hours for Google to update
4. Clear your browser cache and search again

### Job disappeared from Google

**Common causes:**
- Job expired (45-day limit)
- Job was closed or deleted
- Unpublished from Job Boards tab
- Google detected policy violations

**Solution:**
- Renew expired jobs
- Republish if accidentally unpublished
- Ensure job complies with Google's policies

## Google for Jobs Policies

Your job must comply with Google's policies:

### ❌ Prohibited Content

- Discriminatory language
- Misleading information
- Scam or fraudulent jobs
- Jobs requiring upfront payment
- Multi-level marketing (MLM) schemes

### ✅ Best Practices

- Be honest and accurate
- Use clear, professional language
- Provide complete information
- Update jobs regularly
- Remove filled positions promptly

<Callout type="warn">
**Warning**: Jobs that violate Google's policies may be removed and could affect your ability to publish future jobs.

## Common Questions

### Is Google for Jobs free?

Yes! Publishing to Google for Jobs through Flowxtra is completely free. There are no hidden costs or fees.

### How long does it take to appear on Google?

Typically **24-48 hours** after publishing. Some jobs may appear sooner, others may take up to 72 hours.

### Can I publish multiple jobs to Google?

Yes! You can publish as many jobs as you want. Each job is indexed separately.

### Do I need a Google account?

No. Flowxtra handles all the technical integration with Google for Jobs automatically.

### Will my job appear in all countries?

Your job appears in Google search results globally, but it's most visible to searchers in your specified location.

### Can I edit my job after publishing to Google?

Yes! Any edits you make in Flowxtra automatically sync to Google for Jobs within 24-48 hours.

### What if my job expires?

When your job expires (after 45 days), it's automatically removed from Google for Jobs. You can renew it to republish.

### Can I see how many people found my job on Google?

Yes! Check the Analytics section to see traffic sources, including Google.
