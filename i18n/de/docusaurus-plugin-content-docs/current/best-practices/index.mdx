## Top 3 Tips for Success

1. **Be Transparent**: Include salary ranges. Jobs with salaries get 30% more applications.
2. **Be Fast**: Respond to candidates within 48 hours to secure top talent.
3. **Be Mobile-Friendly**: Keep application forms short (resume + email only) for mobile applicants.
